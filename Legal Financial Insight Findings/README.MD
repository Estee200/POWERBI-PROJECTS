##  Project Overview

Working in a law firm gave me insight into the financial side of legal operations, from billing and collections to revenue reporting. Inspired by these processes, I built this project to demonstrate how Business Intelligence can help law firms monitor financial performance, improve collections, and support better decision-making.

Using a realistic synthetic dataset generated with Python, I cleaned and transformed the data using SQL before developing an interactive Power BI dashboard to deliver actionable financial insights.

---

##  Business Problem

Law firms generate large volumes of billing data, but without effective reporting, it can be difficult to answer important business questions such as:

- How much revenue has been billed and collected?
- Which practice areas generate the most revenue?
- Which attorneys contribute the highest billable revenue?
- Which clients still have outstanding balances?
- Which regions are driving the firm's financial performance?

This project demonstrates how Business Intelligence can transform legal billing data into meaningful insights that support financial decision-making.

---

##  Dataset

To simulate a real-world legal finance environment, I generated a synthetic dataset containing over **5,000 legal billing records** using Python with inconsistent, duplicate, invalid date, negative and missing values.

The dataset includes:

- Invoice Information
- Client Details
- Attorney Information
- Practice Areas
- Billing Types
- Revenue & Collections
- Write-Offs
- Outstanding Balances
- Payment Timelines

The data was then cleaned and transformed using SQL before being imported into Power BI.

#  Key Insights

- Collection performance remains below 100%, highlighting opportunities to improve cash flow.
- Revenue is well distributed across regions, with Abuja Office contributing the highest share.
- A small number of attorneys generate the largest share of billable revenue.
- Outstanding balances remain an important area for revenue recovery.

---

#  Recommendations

- Strengthen collection efforts on outstanding invoices.
- Monitor attorney and practice area performance regularly.
- Use financial insights to improve billing efficiency and support strategic business decisions.
